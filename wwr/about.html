Rapids University

Purpose: To provide thrilling and safe rafting experiences.
Mission: To be the leading rafting company in the region.
Creed: Creed of One Body, One Purpose We are many, yet we are one. One body, one spirit, one mission. Each member sacred, each role essential. No hand moves without the arm, No heart beats without the breath, No soul rises without the strength of all. We do not walk alone. We lift, we listen, we labor together. When one suffers, we all feel. When one rejoices, we all rise. We are called to build, not break— To heal, not harm— To serve, not stand still. Bound by love, driven by faith, We honor the divine design: That no part is forgotten, No gift is wasted, No voice is silent. This is our creed. This is our covenant. We are one body— And we move as one.
Motto: "One Body, One Soul, Forged in the Chaos of the Rapids!"
