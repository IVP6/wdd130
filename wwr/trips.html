Rapids University Trips

Our Rafting Trips

At Rapids University, we offer a variety of rafting trips tailored to different skill levels and interests. Whether you're a beginner looking for a fun day on the water or an experienced rafter seeking thrilling rapids, we have something for everyone.

Beginner's Bliss

Duration: Half-day
Difficulty: Easy
Description: Perfect for first-timers, this trip offers gentle rapids and beautiful scenery. Our experienced guides will ensure you have a safe and enjoyable experience.

Intermediate Adventure

Duration: Full-day
Difficulty: Moderate
Description: For those with some rafting experience, this trip features exciting rapids and stunning landscapes. Enjoy a mix of thrill and relaxation as you navigate the river.

Advanced Expedition

Duration: Multi-day
Difficulty: Hard
Description: Designed for seasoned rafters, this expedition takes you through challenging white-water rapids and remote wilderness areas. Prepare for an adrenaline-pumping adventure!
